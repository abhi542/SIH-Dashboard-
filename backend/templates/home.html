Interactive Skill Enhancer (ISE) for Children with ASD and ID

Empowering children with ASD and ID through gamified VR experiences

What is ASD and Intellectual Disabilities (ID)?

ASD: A neurodevelopmental condition affecting social interactions and communication.

ID: Intellectual challenges affecting cognitive tasks and daily living skills.

Our Problem Statement

Children with ASD and ID struggle to develop vital social and conversational skills. Existing tools lack engagement and cultural relevance, leaving these children without sufficient support to build these skills.

Our Solution: Interactive Skills Enhancer (ISE)

ISE is a VR-based application that integrates Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) and speech therapy to create engaging, educational environments. Our solution focuses on:

  • Emotion Recognition Activities
  • Conversational Activities
  • Appropriate Social Responses for Problem Solving
  • Collaborative Activities
  • Feedback with Positive Reinforcement
  • Sensory Responses

By using VR and gamification, we make learning interpersonal skills enjoyable and effective. This innovative solution helps children practice social interactions in a safe, interactive space.
